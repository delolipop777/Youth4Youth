About Us

Youth4Youth Global was created because many young people need support, understanding and trust. We believe every young person has ideas, knowledge and care to share. Our motto is "Let's help each other" - and we're taking action on that belief.

What makes us different is our focus on small but heartfelt steps - a book shared, advice given, a minute of time - that can mean the world to someone. We believe in the power of youth to create positive change when we unite.

Our Mission

To unite and support young people, expand their opportunities, and implement positive changes in society. Every young person's thoughts, desires and actions can contribute to social progress.

Our Vision

A world where youth support each other through challenges in education, health and employment, where every voice is heard, and where we collaborate to build a brighter future together.
